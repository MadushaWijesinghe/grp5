Brass Crafts in Sri Lanka

       

Brass Crafts Industry

Areas such as Pilimatalawa and Kiriwavula in Kandy are best known for their silver and brass works. These brass and silver items are used to decorate temple buildings, furniture and various items such as Statues, Sword handles, jewellery and lamps.

To make these products even more attractive, local craftsmen engrave the brassware with Traditional Sinhala ornaments such as Liyawel, Bherunda Pakshiya, Hansa Puttuwa, Ira handa, Palapethi, Nelum Pethi. The brassware items available today are wide and varied and includes oil lamps, trays, bowls, vases, betel holders, jewellery boxes, letter openers, bottle openers and candle holders.
